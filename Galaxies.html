Galaxies around us

Andromeda

A barred spiral galaxy approximately 2.5 million light-years (770 kiloparsecs) from Earth and the nearest galaxy to the Milky Way.

Antennae

This Galaxies are a pair of interacting galaxies in the constellation Corvus. They are currently going through a starburst phase.

Milky Way

The Milky Way is the galaxy that includes the Solar System, with the name describing the galaxy's appearance from Earth

Whirlpool

The Whirlpool Galaxy is an interacting grand-design spiral galaxy with a Seyfert 2 active galactic nucleus.

Circinus

This Galaxy is a Seyfert galaxy in the constellation of Circinus. It is located 4 degrees below the Galactic plane, and, at a distance of 4.0 Mpc.

Messier 82

This galaxy also known asCigar is a starburst galaxy approximately 12 million light-years away in the constellation Ursa Major.
